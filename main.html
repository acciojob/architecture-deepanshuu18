Architectural Design Overview

This section explains the architectural design principles, focusing on sustainable building materials and techniques used to reduce the environmental impact of construction. It discusses modern trends such as smart homes, energy-efficient systems, and the integration of nature into urban designs.

The second section focuses on the importance of architectural aesthetics. From classical styles to modern minimalism, this part highlights key design elements such as symmetry, proportion, and form. The role of architectural beauty in influencing both function and emotional well-being is also explored.
